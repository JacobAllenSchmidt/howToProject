Making Pizza A La Jacob!

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust, homemade or store-bought,
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pecans or Walnuts, preferably in peices
  • Spinach
  • Peperocini peppers
  • Banana peppers
  • Olives
  • Mozzarella

The end result! Doesn't it look delicious?

Instructions

  1. Generously spread pizza sauce over all the crust
  2. Spread the pecans/walnuts over the crust
  3. Spread spinach over the nuts
  4. Spread the banana peppers and peperocini over the spinach
  5. Cut the olives in half
  6. Spread the olives over everything else
  7. Generously spread cheese over everything
  8. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes. Let cool for 2 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
